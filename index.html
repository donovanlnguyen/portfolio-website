    Education

    B. Sc Bachelors Degree Biology

    My name is Donovan Nguyen and I graduated from California State University, Fullerton with a Bachelors of Science in Biology. My interest in coding stemmed from when I worked at a clinical research lab. The lab's objective was to find a non-invasive way to read blood glucose. As a lab technician, I also worked with software engineers on the project. I had the opportunity to learn Linux and learned how to run the programs that the software engineers had created. These programs worked in tandem with the experiments we were running in the lab, which sparked my interest in learning about programming. At the start of the pandemic, our lab was shut down because the patients we worked with were at-risk due to COVID. Following the shutdown, I decided I wanted to further pursue my interest in coding and programming which began my journey towards transitioning into a software engineer.
